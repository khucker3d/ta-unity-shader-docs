# Tech Art - My Unity Shader Journey

## About:
My journey with shader programming began in late 2014 through online tutorials. Early on, I found the process challenging, but that changed in 2016 when I discovered Freya Holmer’s Shader Forge. By 2018, I transitioned to Unity’s URP Shader Graph, where node-based workflows helped me visualize shader logic and build a strong foundational understanding of how shaders are constructed. In late 2023, I returned to writing CG/HLSL directly, focusing on Unity BRP and URP UI shader FX. This shift allowed me to combine visual prototyping with code-driven control, resulting in more flexible, optimized, and production-ready shader systems.
